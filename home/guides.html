Season by Season

Best Time to Visit

Las Vegas has 300+ sunny days a year — but timing still matters.

🌸

Spring (Mar–May)

Best overall. Mild temps (65–85°F), fewer crowds, outdoor activities shine.

☀️

Summer (Jun–Aug)

Peak tourism + scorching heat (110°F+). Great for pool parties, hotel deals indoors.

🍂

Fall (Sept–Nov)

Second-best season. Cooler, less crowded, excellent for outdoor day trips.

❄️

Winter (Dec–Feb)

Cheapest rates, crisp air, zero crowds. Holiday season brings dazzling decorations.
